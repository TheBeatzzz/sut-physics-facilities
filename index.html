A clear path from

Whether you are beginning a student project or planning a cross-disciplinary study, start with the research question. The facility team can help identify the right method.

  1. 01

    Share your question

    Describe the sample, signal, or phenomenon you need to investigate.

  2. 02

    Plan the method

    Discuss feasibility, preparation, training, scheduling, and data needs.

  3. 03

    Run with support

    Work safely with an instrument specialist and record a reproducible method.
